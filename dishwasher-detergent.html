Dishwasher Detergent

Best Practices

These are the things that make up a good sustainable dishwasher detergent.

  • sustainable packaging
  • nontoxic ingredients
  • 0 microplastics!
Worst Practices

These are the things that make up an unsustainable dishwasher detergent.

  • large single use plastic jugs
  • dishwasher "pods" enclosed in single use plastic
  • toxic ingredients like phosphates and surfactants
